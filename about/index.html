About this site

KansalaisuusKoe.org is an independent, non-official informational resource focusing on civic knowledge related to Finland. The site is intended to provide structured, accessible explanations of topics that commonly arise in the context of citizenship, integration, and civic participation.

The content published on this site is based on publicly available information, including Finnish law, institutional structures, historical sources, and general civic education materials. It is presented for informational and educational purposes only.

No part of this site represents official guidance, legal advice, or an authoritative interpretation of current or future citizenship requirements. The site is not affiliated with Finnish authorities or public institutions.

The structure of the site reflects widely recognised areas of civic knowledge, such as democratic institutions, fundamental rights and duties, public administration, social structures, and everyday life in Finland. These areas are explained through articles and illustrative questions designed to support understanding and self-assessment.

Information related to citizenship legislation and policy developments is included only insofar as it has been made public through official communications or widely reported sources. Where details are incomplete or subject to change, this is explicitly noted.

The site is maintained as a general educational resource. Content may be updated, reorganised, or expanded over time in response to changes in legislation, public guidance, or the availability of new information.

This site does not collect personal data for identification purposes, does not provide personalised assessments, and does not replace official procedures or requirements.

If you notice factual inaccuracies or outdated information, you may contact the site for the purpose of correction or clarification.
